Online Examinations

Online examinations are examinations that are conducted by computer or mobile and its various technologies and multimedia, or via the Internet, to measure and evaluate the level of students’ performance in a subject or course, so that they are automatically corrected and monitored, which ensures credibility and transparency in the correction and saves time and effort at the homeland (Somaliland).

And these are online examinations that are identical to those of traditional paper-based exams, in terms of the number of questions, their order, the degree of difficulty and the way they are presented.

ERP Examinations System

All modern examinations use multiple software tools and professional management system to make examinations in all educational institutions efficiently and effectively. When you look for a single system that connects all the examinations of all educational institutions, you will understand the significance of the educational management information system (ERP Examinations System) working as the backbone of modern examinations.

And facilitate the tasks of the main government educational institutions, and they are: the Ministry of Education and the Ministry of Higher Education in (Somaliland). ERP Examinations System is a cloud computing system to unify and manage private and public examinations in the homeland (Somaliland).this is the most powerful tool to build smart examinations.
